---
name: Sharper commercial metrics — booking completion not link delivery
description: Sprint success metrics should measure actual outcomes (booked) not intermediate steps (link delivered)
type: feedback
---

"30%+ of qualified leads receive a booking link" is weak. Receiving a link is not booking. The real proof is qualified-to-booked, not qualified-to-link.

**Why:** Link delivery measures plumbing, not commercial value. A lead can get a link and never click it. The wedge is only real if leads actually book.

**How to apply:** Track "booking intent converted to booked outcome" as the real metric, even if manually confirmed during pilot. Keep link delivery as a diagnostic metric, not a pass criterion.
