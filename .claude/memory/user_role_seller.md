User is not the SMB owner/operator. He is the one selling Switchboard to SMB owners. The SMB owners and their teams are the actual end users of the dashboard and agent pages.

Design decisions should optimize for SMB owner comprehension (busy, non-technical, "is my agent earning its keep?") and team member productivity (approve, act, review).
