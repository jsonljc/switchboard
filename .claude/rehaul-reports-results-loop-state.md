- T3.1 (create reports-shared/ + move format.ts + repoint imports) is the ONLY purely-autonomous slice (pure-function move, no rendering change). DEFER the mercury-voice.module.css extraction out of T3.1 (it is entangled with the /results re-skin).

  Sequencing options for T3.2+: (a) voice-first (re-skin /results to mercury wholesale [surfaced], THEN dedup the now-identical widgets trivially), or (b) per-widget (each shared-widget extraction in mercury voice re-voices /results' widget [surfaced incrementally]). RECOMMEND (b) per-widget incremental (smaller, reviewable diffs) but confirm with user at T3.2.
