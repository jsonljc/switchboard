# Acme Fitness: Communication Style

<!-- WORKED EXAMPLE: fictional. -->

```yaml
per_contact:
  - contact: Jane Tan
    channel: email
    register: formal-short
    format: "bullets over prose; max ~150 words; one ask per email"
    language: English
    do: ["lead with their numbers", "name the effort cost honestly", "offer a small next step"]
    dont: ["no emojis", "no 'quick question'", "no platform-push framing ('Meta recommends...')"]

  - contact: Marcus Lim
    channel: whatsapp
    register: casual-direct
    format: "2-4 short messages max; link instead of attach"
    language: English
    do: ["technical detail welcome", "screenshots help"]
    dont: ["no walls of text", "nothing Jane hasn't seen if it implies budget"]

global_rules:
  - "numbers always carry a timeframe and source"
  - "never reference other clients, even anonymized, unless from the cleared benchmark library"

cadence:
  follow_up_after_no_reply: 4 days
  max_unanswered_touches: 3 (then switch channel once, then park)
```
